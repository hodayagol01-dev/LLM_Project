# Music and Sound Prompt Documentation: Assignment 06

## 1. Introduction

This document details the prompts designed for an AI music and sound generator to create the audio landscape for the ShoreStep AI video. The goal is to produce a dynamic soundtrack that enhances the narrative's emotional arc, transitioning from tension and anxiety to calm, clarity, and empowerment. All sound effects will be used to enrich the visual storytelling and user experience.

## 2. General Audio Vibe

*   **Overall Arc:** Start with slightly tense, anxious tones, transition to serene and calming, and conclude with uplifting and confident.
*   **Instrumentation:** Favor synthetic pads, gentle piano, and natural ambient sounds (ocean waves, seagulls) for calm; subtle dissonant synths, muted percussion, or minor piano keys for tension.
*   **Pacing:** Match the visual pacing, starting frantic, moving to slow and deliberate, then concluding with a moderate, positive pace.

## 3. Scene-Specific Audio Prompts

### 3.1. Scene: `anxiety_apartment_night` (0-10 seconds)

*   **Music Prompt:** "Compose a 10-second track. Start with tense, slightly dissonant piano chords in a minor key, gradually building a subtle, low-frequency synthetic drone. The music should evoke a feeling of stress and mental overwhelm, with a slightly erratic tempo to reflect anxiety. End with a subtle, uneasy lingering chord." 
*   **Sound Effects Prompt:** "Generate distinct sounds for 'frantic keyboard typing' (rapid, slightly aggressive keystrokes) and 'nervous sighs' (short, sharp intakes of breath followed by a quick exhalation). Ensure these sounds are prominent but not overpowering the music or narration."

### 3.2. Scene: `anxiety_eyes_closed_transition` (10-20 seconds)

*   **Music Prompt:** "For this 10-second transition, take the uneasy chord from the previous scene and slowly resolve it into a softer, more ambient pad sound. Introduce a very subtle, ethereal synth shimmer that suggests a shift in perception. The tension should gradually dissipate, leading towards a sense of quiet introspection. No strong beats or melodies." 
*   **Sound Effects Prompt:** "Minimal. Perhaps a very subtle, almost subliminal, 'blur' or 'whoosh' sound effect that accompanies the visual warping, fading quickly. No dialogue."

### 3.3. Scene: `serene_beach_day` (20-35 seconds)

*   **Music Prompt:** "Compose a 15-second track. Begin with a seamless transition from the ambient pad of the previous scene. Introduce a calming, flowing synth pad melody with a slow, gentle tempo. Incorporate subtle, major-key piano arpeggios that evoke peace and clarity. The music should feel expansive and tranquil, like an open beach, and gradually swell in warmth. Ensure no sharp changes." 
*   **Sound Effects Prompt:** "Generate realistic, gentle 'ocean waves' lapping at a shore. Include 'distant seagulls' calls, appearing intermittently and subtly. The soundscape should be immersive and peaceful, allowing the music and narration to layer on top naturally."

### 3.4. Scene: `shorestep_ai_interface` (35-45 seconds)

*   **Music Prompt:** "Continue the calm, flowing synth pad and gentle piano from the beach scene, but subtly introduce a very light, rhythmic, almost digital pulse (e.g., a soft, unobtrusive click or subtle arpeggiated synth) that suggests focus and gentle productivity. Maintain the overall calming and confident tone for 10 seconds. The music should feel supportive and encouraging." 
*   **Sound Effects Prompt:** "Generate 'calm, focused keyboard typing' sounds (even, measured keystrokes, less aggressive than before). Include 'gentle UI chimes' or 'soft confirmation sounds' for visual feedback when the AI processes and displays output. These should be clean and unobtrusive."

### 3.5. Scene: `navigating_structured_docs` (45-55 seconds)

*   **Music Prompt:** "Maintain the supportive and gently rhythmic background music from the previous scene for 10 seconds. The music should sustain a feeling of confidence and progress, perhaps with a subtle, positive melodic motif emerging slightly more clearly towards the end, signaling a breakthrough or understanding." 
*   **Sound Effects Prompt:** "Generate a 'subtle page-turn' or 'scroll sound effect' that accompanies the visual navigation through markdown files. It should be crisp but not distracting."

### 3.6. Scene: `climax_and_call_to_action` (55-60 seconds)

*   **Music Prompt:** "For the final 5 seconds, let the calming, positive music swell slightly, becoming more uplifting and conclusive. End with a clear, resonant, and inspiring chord that fades out cleanly, leaving a feeling of empowerment and completion." 
*   **Sound Effects Prompt:** "No specific new sound effects, but ensure the music's swelling complements the quick cuts and the final logo reveal. The overall audio should lead to a strong, positive conclusion."
