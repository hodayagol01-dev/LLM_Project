# Product Requirements Document (PRD): ShoreStep AI

## 1. Introduction

ShoreStep AI is a conceptual AI-powered assistant designed to help students overcome anxiety and overwhelm when faced with complex academic assignments. By guiding them through a structured planning process, ShoreStep AI aims to transform feelings of stress into clarity and actionable steps. This PRD outlines the product's vision, features, and user experience for its initial multimedia demonstration.

## 2. Vision

To empower students to approach academic challenges with confidence and clarity, by providing an intuitive AI-driven tool that structures complex tasks into manageable, actionable plans.

## 3. Goals

*   **Reduce Student Anxiety:** Provide a calming and structured approach to assignment planning.
*   **Enhance Productivity:** Enable students to quickly break down assignments into actionable components.
*   **Demonstrate AI Capabilities:** Showcase the effectiveness of AI agents in generating structured documentation and multimedia content.
*   **Illustrate Vibe Coding:** Visually represent the transformation of an emotional state ("overwhelmed") into a structured plan and multimedia output.

## 4. User Story (as depicted in the video)

*   **As an overwhelmed student,** I want a tool that helps me de-stress and organize my academic assignments, so I can feel calm and in control.
*   **As an AI agent developer,** I want to demonstrate how AI can take a high-level creative brief and generate a detailed script, scene structure, and ultimately, multimedia code.

## 5. Key Features (demonstrated in the video)

*   **Assignment Breakdown:** AI-driven decomposition of a complex assignment into core components (PRD, PLAN, TODO).
*   **Prompt Generation:** Automated creation of effective prompts for subsequent AI tasks.
*   **Structured Output:** Generation of organized data formats (e.g., JSON scenes, Fountain script).
*   **GitHub Workflow Integration:** Output formatted for seamless integration into a version-controlled project.
*   **Vibe Transformation:** A narrative flow that visually represents the transition from anxiety to calm and clarity.

## 6. Scope of Demonstration (60-second Remotion Video)

The initial scope focuses on a 60-second animated video illustrating the ShoreStep AI concept. The video will visually translate the student's emotional journey and the AI's role in structuring the assignment. It will NOT include a functional ShoreStep AI application but will simulate its outputs.

## 7. Non-Goals

*   Development of a fully functional ShoreStep AI application (beyond the simulated outputs).
*   Complex user authentication or data persistence.
*   Real-time AI interaction within the video itself (video shows *results* of AI interaction).

## 8. Metrics for Success (Video Demonstration)

*   **Clarity of Message:** The video clearly communicates the ShoreStep AI concept and its benefits.
*   **Emotional Resonance:** The video successfully conveys the student's journey from anxiety to calm.
*   **Technical Adherence:** All required deliverables and course requirements are met and clearly demonstrated.
*   **Visual Appeal:** The Remotion video is engaging, polished, and professional.

## 9. Future Considerations (Beyond current scope)

*   Integration with academic platforms (LMS).
*   Advanced natural language processing for deeper assignment analysis.
*   Personalized learning path generation.
*   Multilingual support, including Hebrew/RTL text rendering within the UI/output.
